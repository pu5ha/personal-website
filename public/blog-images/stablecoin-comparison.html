Two Visions for the Future of Money

Centralized
Regulatory
Arbitrage
  • Can be frozen or confiscated
  • Rules set by political discretion
  • Filling a regulatory gap
  • Replaceable by a well-designed CBDC
  • Advantage is temporary
Decentralized
Financial
Sovereignty
  • Cannot be frozen or confiscated
  • Rules enforced by code
  • Redefining the system

  • Freedom technology
